Old Style Totem Maker 😛

Type your Minecraft username to instantly generate and download your custom old-style totem texture pack!


How to Use?

  1. Type your username and click "Download Texture Pack".
  2. Copy the downloaded .zip file without extracting it.
  3. Open Minecraft, go to Options > Resource Packs > Open Pack Folder.
  4. Paste the file there, activate it in-game, and enjoy your classic custom totem!
